Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2023

1. Information Collection

a. Account Creation: When you create an account on PassMan, we collect your email address and a securely encrypted version of your password. We do not have access to your actual password.

b. Password Storage: PassMan securely stores the passwords you save in your account, using strong encryption techniques. We do not have access to your stored passwords as they are encrypted and can only be decrypted by you.

2. Information Usage

a. Personalization: PassMan uses the provided email address to personalize your account and provide a tailored experience.

b. Communication: We may occasionally send you service-related emails, such as password reset notifications or important updates about PassMan. You can choose to opt out of these communications.

3. Information Sharing

a. User Data: PassMan does not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

b. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.

4. Third-Party Services

a. Integration: PassMan may integrate with third-party services, such as password strength checkers or password generators, to enhance your experience. However, these services are subject to their own privacy policies and terms of use.
